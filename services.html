Our Services

We offer complete "Pin to Pin" construction services, managing every aspect of your project from start to finish.

  • Planning & Design: Architectural planning, 2D/3D elevation, structural design, site inspection, soil testing.
  • Foundation & Civil Work: Excavation, footing, column/slab casting, wall construction, plastering.
  • Electrical Works: House wiring, DB board setup, lighting, CCTV installation.
  • Plumbing & Sanitary: Water pipelines, drainage, bathroom fittings, tank/sump setup.
  • Carpentry & Woodwork: Doors, kitchen cabinets, wardrobes, window panels.
  • Flooring & Tiling: Vitrified/granite/marble tiles, bathroom tiling, skirting.
  • Painting & Finishing: Internal/external painting, texture painting, wood polish.
  • Grills, Gates, Fabrication: Main gate, window grills, stair/balcony railings, pergola.
  • Exterior & Extra Work: Compound wall, landscaping, borewell, paving, waterproofing.
  • Final Handover: Site cleaning, testing, walkthrough, key handover.
